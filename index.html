Welcome to the Starting Line!

TrialReady is your friendly mobile guide, designed specifically for individuals like you who are new to the exciting world of dog agility competitions. We know starting out can feel overwhelming – that's why we built this app to make the process simple, clear, and encouraging.

Demystify the Agility Journey

Our mission is to take the confusion out of entering your first agility trial. No more sifting through complex websites or feeling lost in jargon. We provide the essential information you need, right at your fingertips.

  • Focus on the fun with your furry friend, not the frustrating paperwork.
  • Build confidence as you learn the ropes of agility competition.
